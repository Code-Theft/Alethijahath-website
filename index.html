About
ethijahath

Al Ethijahath is a new generation company which starts its operation since 2004 with high skilled professionals to serve our valuable customers. We are offering unrivalled services in the field of heavy plants, warehouses and logistics etc across Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
Al Ethijahath work with a huge variety of customers in Saudi Arabia to Supply, installation and maintenance of heavy equipments and machineries including different kind of manufacturing machines, forklifts, boilers, coolers, batteries and genuine spare parts for all branded equipments and machines. Our team are proficient in installing, modernization and maintenance of all kind of heavy equipments and machines as per the customers’ requirements.
